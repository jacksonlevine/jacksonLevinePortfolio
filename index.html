About Me:

My name is Jackson, I am a 23-year-old programming enthusiast! I have worked various jobs including landscaping, cashier work, and food service, but have become more excited by programming over the last few years, at first just wanting to type on a keyboard for fun, but soon in awe of what can be done with relatively easy logic that felt intuitive to me!

This was my first time realizing that you can make a program that does anything! Being inspired by my years spent playing Minecraft, I attempted to write a similar game using the C# console. I later moved on to Unity Game Engine and continued learning more ways to code in the process!

Aside from coding, I am a lover of music and coffee. I wake up every day with a song that I need to hear that day, I couldn't do without it!
